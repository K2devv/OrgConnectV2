YOUTH EMPOWERMENT SUMMIT 2024: LEADERSHIP AND INNOVATION

EVENT OVERVIEW

The Youth Empowerment Summit 2024 was a highly successful event, drawing over 200 students to participate in a day of leadership development and technology innovation. Organized by the Student Development and Leadership Organization (SDLO) in collaboration with the Tech Innovators Club (TIC), the summit provided students with the opportunity to enhance their leadership skills while exploring emerging technologies.

The event featured a keynote address by a well-known entrepreneur who discussed the role of leadership in the digital age. Students also took part in various workshops covering topics such as leadership, public speaking, and innovation, all led by industry professionals and student leaders.

As part of the event, participants worked together on collaboration projects that challenged them to find creative solutions to real-world problems using leadership principles and technology. The summit was a great success, fostering teamwork and innovation among students, and strengthening the partnership between SDLO and TIC.
